FAQ

Q1: What types of events do you cater for?
We cater for weddings, corporate functions, private parties, funerals, training sessions, conferences, and more.

Q2: Do you provide vegetarian, vegan, or halaal options?
Yes! We create tailored menus to suit dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, and halaal-friendly meals.

Q3: Can you help with more than just food?
Absolutely. We also provide crockery, cutlery, glassware, linen, tables, chairs, staff, bar services, décor, and marquee hire.

Q4: What areas do you serve?
We are based in Centurion and serve Pretoria, Midrand, Johannesburg, and surrounding areas.

Q5: Do you offer tasting sessions before big events?
Yes, tasting sessions can be arranged for weddings or large corporate events.

Q6: What is your minimum order size?
We cater for groups as small as 10 people and up to large events with hundreds of guests.

Q7: How do I get a quote?
You can email us at info@real-food.co.za, call our team, or use the contact form on our website.
